### OnePage Studio Pricing

| Plan | Monthly price | Key limits | Included features |
| :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Starter | Free | Up to 2 pages (Lynx), 1 Food Menu, basic templates only | All core templates, basic analytics (views), QR codes for menus, email notifications for comments |
| Creator | \$12 / month | Up to 5 pages (Lynx), 2 Food Menus, standard templates | Everything in Starter, custom branding (logo, colors), advanced Food Menu features (multi‑location, multi‑contact, product variations) |
| Studio | \$29 / month | Up to 15 pages, unlimited Food Menus | Everything in Creator, advanced analytics (per‑page and per‑template metrics), exportable data, higher email notification limits |
